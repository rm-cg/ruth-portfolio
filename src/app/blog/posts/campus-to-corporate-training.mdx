## The "Let's Open Doors" Experience

As I prepare to transition from my BS Mathematics studies at PLM into the professional tech industry, I recently completed a 2-month **Campus to Corporate Training** hosted by RR Donnelley Philippines Inc. 

What surprised me most about this experience was how hands-on and incredibly patient the instructors were. Instead of just lecturing, they emphasized the common mistakes people make in the workplace and showed us exactly how to navigate them. We underwent comprehensive training on using Google Workspace efficiently, right down to mastering essential keyboard shortcuts that save precious time during daily tasks. 

A standout mentor for me was Ms. Ella Pabico, whose articulate and patient teaching style made the transition much easier to digest. I also highly appreciated the mock interview practices they conducted. Going through simulated corporate interviews significantly boosted my professional confidence and taught me how to articulate my thoughts under pressure.

## Mastering Resumes and Presentations

Technical skills alone aren't enough to thrive as a Data Analyst. This program focused heavily on corporate culture, professional communication, and the actual deliverables expected in a workplace.

One of the most valuable modules was on **Resume Writing**. We learned how to completely shift from an academic, student-focused mindset to a metrics-driven, professional one. The instructors taught us the industry standards for highlighting transferable skills, structuring our experiences cleanly, and quantifying our impact so that our applications actually stand out to corporate recruiters.

Another unexpectedly game-changing part of the training was the deep dive into **presentation skills**, specifically focusing on often-overlooked techniques in Microsoft PowerPoint. As an aspiring Data Analyst, knowing how to analyze data is only half the battle; knowing how to visually present it clearly to stakeholders is the other. We learned how to avoid visual clutter, properly structure our slides for corporate audiences, and utilize advanced formatting and storytelling techniques that go way beyond the basic default templates.

## Building Professional Readiness

This training completely reshaped my view on workplace dynamics. I realized that as long as you are approachable and foster good camaraderie with your colleagues, you can navigate any challenge smoothly. Building proper, positive relationships in the workplace doesn't just make the environment better, but it genuinely makes you excited to go to work and collaborate every single day.

## Looking Ahead

Completing this training has made me feel incredibly prepared to step into a corporate environment. I am excited to combine the soft skills, presentation techniques, and professional etiquette I learned here with my technical background in data to make a real impact on day one.
