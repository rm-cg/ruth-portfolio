## Overview

As part of my continuous growth in Data Analytics, I am currently laying the groundwork for an exploratory data analysis project centered around **Urban Heat Islands (UHI)**. In rapidly urbanizing areas like Metro Manila, built-up surfaces generate warmer local temperatures, intensifying thermal stress. 

Instead of just looking at theoretical numbers, my goal is to extract satellite environmental data, clean it, and build clear, actionable visualizations to map these heat islands and identify the most vulnerable barangays.

## The Data Analytics Approach

I am approaching this project by applying the core pillars of data analytics:

- **Data Extraction & Structuring:** I am learning how to pull and process complex environmental datasets, such as Land Surface Temperature (LST) retrieved from satellite imagery, and structure them into relational databases using SQL.
- **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Applying descriptive statistics to calculate the mean land surface temperatures across different cities and identifying significant temperature differences and outliers.
- **Data Visualization & Dashboarding:** My ultimate deliverable for this project is to translate this raw data into clear, user-friendly heatmaps and dashboards. This will allow stakeholders to easily digest the information and make data-driven decisions for climate-responsive urban planning.
